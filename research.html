Research Projects & Grants

My academic inquiry revolves around environmental social science, with a specific focus on the intersection of physical environments and human well-being.

Climate-Induced Migration

Exploring the social and economic dynamics of communities displaced by environmental changes and structural adaptation measures.

Climate Change Adaptation

Evaluating strategies and policy interventions aimed at building resilience in vulnerable populations.

WASH

Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene interventions and their impact on public health and poverty alleviation.

Quality of Life

Measuring multi-dimensional poverty and the holistic well-being of targeted demographics in development programs.
