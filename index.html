Hi! Nice to meet you!

I am Sharan, currently conducting research in the nexus of Climate Change, Migration, WASH and Quality of Life.

Recent Professional Commitments

  • Senior Research Associate

    Environment and Climate Change Research Group, BRAC Institute of Governance and Development, Bangladesh.

  • Senior Analyst

    Research & Evaluation Unit, Poverty Alleviation Cluster, BRAC.

  • Graduate Teaching Assistant

    Master of Development Studies, BRAC University.

Trained as a Water Resource Engineer at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), I worked briefly in the sector of water networks at the Bangladesh Water Development Board. With an ambition to engage and contribute more in the environmental social science knowledge domain, I pursued a Master of Development Studies.

My current research focuses on climate-induced migration, climate change adaptation, WASH, and Quality of Life. I also contribute to teaching a master level program at BRAC University.

In my (hardly) free time, I am learning photography & try doing creative writing. I love travelling and meeting new people, learning from their experiences.
